# (working name)

A mobile-first pet health management app that helps owners track, understand, and act on their pet’s health.

---

## ✨ Overview

Our app is built to simplify pet care. Instead of scattered notes, vet papers, and missed reminders, everything lives in one place.

Track your pet’s health, get smart insights from your data, and take action when it matters.

> This project focuses on building a real, production-style system — not just a demo.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🐶 **Pet Profiles**
  - Manage multiple pets with detailed health history

- 💊 **Health Tracking**
  - Medications, vaccinations, weight, diet, symptoms

- 🔔 **Smart Reminders**
  - Never miss meds, feeding times, or vet visits

- 🧠 **AI Pattern Detection**
  - Surfaces trends like weight changes or recurring symptoms  
  - *Descriptive only — not medical advice* :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

- 🗺️ **Vet Locator**
  - Find nearby clinics instantly

- 📄 **Health Record Export**
  - Share full history with your vet
